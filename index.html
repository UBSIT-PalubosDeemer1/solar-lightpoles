Frequently Asked Questions

Product Questions

How do the solar panels work during cloudy days?

Our solar panels are designed with high-efficiency cells that can capture diffused sunlight on cloudy days. Additionally, each unit includes a battery backup system that stores excess energy during sunny periods to ensure continuous operation.

How often do the air filters need replacement?

The air purification filters typically need replacement every 6-12 months, depending on local air quality conditions. Our systems include indicator lights that notify maintenance teams when filter replacement is needed.

What maintenance is required for the light poles?

Our solar light poles require minimal maintenance. We recommend visual inspections every 3 months and a comprehensive maintenance check annually. Our team offers maintenance packages to ensure optimal performance.

Order & Shipping

What is the delivery time for orders?

Standard delivery takes 2-4 weeks after order confirmation. For urgent projects, we offer expedited shipping options that can reduce delivery time to 1-2 weeks (additional charges apply).

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our products. Our certified technicians ensure proper installation and system optimization. Installation services can be added during the ordering process.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a 5-year warranty on all our solar light poles, covering manufacturing defects and performance issues. The solar panels come with a 25-year performance warranty, and batteries are covered for 3 years.
